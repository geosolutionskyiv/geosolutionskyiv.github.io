About company

“Geomatics Solutions” Company was established in 2002 by a group of specialists in the field of Geomatics. For 23 years, the company has been well proven in production and in delivery of services related to the creation, processing, and use of geospatial data for solving various practical problems.

Services offered by the company

  • Creation of geospatial data sets designed to build information management systems for distributed objects
  • Disasters risk modeling
  • Production of cartographic and geodetic products
  • Implementation of large-scale (1:200-1:2000) topographic surveys and other types of topographic and geodetic works
  • Creation of topographic maps databases (scale 1:10 000–100,000; 1:1,000,000)
  • Collection of topographic, geodetic and cartographic data for the creation and maintenance of cadastral systems
  • Laser scanning and 3D modeling
  • Stereophotogrammetric work and processing of remote sensing data
  • Creation and support of topographic, geodetic and mapping digital databases
  • Research and implementation of projects in the field of geodesy, cartography, and cadastre
  • Development of application software in the field of GIS and management of GIS projects
  • Consultancy support in the use of geospatial data, work with various hardware and software in GIS and digital photogrammetry, solving problems in cadastre and real estate valuation systems
  • Delivery of software, digital maps, databases, and remote sensing data
  • Field quality control of topographic-geodetic and cadastral works
  • Development of topographic, thematic, environmental, cadastral, and other maps, plans, atlases, and digital elevation models
  • Organizing and conducting training courses and workshops in theoretical and practical GIS technologies for collecting and processing geospatial data, training with various geodetic and photogrammetric equipment, GPS receivers
  • Consulting services in the field of Geomatics
  • Monitoring and certification of objects of cultural heritage

Licenses

  • The license of the State Land Agency of Ukraine
  • The license of the State Service of Geodesy, Cartography, and Cadastre
  • ISO 9001-2015 on the quality control system works

Partnership

The company's partners: “Analityka” LLC (Ukraine), Intelligence Systems “Geo” (Ukraine).

Staff

“GeoSolutions” Ltd. has 25 employees, including 1 Ph.D. in Technical Sciences.

The company staff has a vast experience of participation in various domestic and international projects related to the geospatial data creation and processing, creation of pilot systems of real estate registration and cadastre in Ukraine, training courses in GIT development.

Main Activities

  • development of application software
  • consulting services in the field of Geomatics

  • processing of remote sensing data
  • preparing and conducting training courses in the field of Geomatics

Techno-economic Potential and Experience
